About Us

Buddys Pizza is amongst the top picks of the locals to satiate cravings for mouth-watering pizzas. The restaurant specializes in serving a range of cheesy pizzas made with the goodness of hand-picked toppings.

Buddy’s Pizza was established in 2016 By Mr.Aashish Joshi. It was started with only one branch and now we have started other branches also. The main branch of Buddy’s pizza is located at Gurukul. We have also franchises which are located at Parimal Cross Road, Bopal and Gandhinagar. The capacity of restaurant is approximately 15 tables or 80 to 85 people. We are now considered in top restaurant because of our services, food quality and their hospitality.
